Cold Coffee

description

Cold coffee is a smooth and refreshing drink that blends milk, sugar, and instant coffee into a chilled, frothy beverage. It’s the perfect choice for hot days when you need an energy boost or just want a cool treat. The creamy texture and mild bitterness of coffee create a perfect balance of taste.

You can customize it with ice cream, chocolate syrup, or whipped cream for extra indulgence. Whether served in a café or made at home, cold coffee never fails to energize and refresh, making it a favorite among coffee lovers of all ages.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp instant coffee
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • Chocolate syrup (optional)

Steps

  1. Add coffee, sugar, and milk to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again for a few seconds.
  4. Pour into a glass and drizzle chocolate syrup on top.
