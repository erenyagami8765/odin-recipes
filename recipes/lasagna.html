Lasagna

description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food known for its rich layers and creamy texture. It’s made by stacking cooked pasta sheets with flavorful tomato sauce, sautéed vegetables or minced meat, and a generous amount of cheese. The combination of sauces and melted cheese gives it a warm, hearty taste that melts in your mouth.

Each layer of lasagna is baked to perfection, with a golden top and soft, cheesy interior. It’s not just a meal but a wholesome experience — ideal for family dinners, special occasions, or anyone craving a filling homemade dish.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (mozzarella & parmesan)
  • Minced meat or vegetables
  • Onion, garlic & olive oil
  • Salt, pepper & herbs

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna sheets until soft.
  2. Cook sauce with onion, garlic, and meat or veggies.
  3. Layer sauce, sheets, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and top with cheese.
  5. Bake until golden and bubbly.
