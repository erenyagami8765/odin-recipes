Pizza

Ingredients

  • dough(flour,yeast,salt,oil,water)
  • Tomato sauce(puree+herbs)
  • mozzarella cheese
  • toppings(capsicum,mushroom,onion,olives)
  • dried oregano
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, and water to make dough.
  2. Rest dough for 1 hour and roll it out.
  3. Spread tomato sauce on the base.
  4. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake till crust is golden and cheese melts.
