Masala Chai

description

Masala chai is a traditional Indian beverage that combines black tea with milk and a mix of aromatic spices. The flavors of ginger, cardamom, and clove blend beautifully to create a warm, soothing drink that instantly refreshes your senses. It’s often enjoyed in the morning or evening with biscuits or snacks.

What makes masala chai special is its comfort and culture. Every household has its own version, but the feeling remains the same — cozy, flavorful, and heartwarming. A cup of chai can lift your mood and bring people together, making it more than just a drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp tea leaves
  • 1-2 tsp sugar
  • Ginger, cardamom, and clove

Steps

  1. Boil water with ginger, cardamom, and clove.
  2. Add tea leaves and let it simmer.
  3. Pour in milk and sugar.
  4. Boil for a few minutes.
  5. Strain and serve hot.
