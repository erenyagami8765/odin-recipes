Masala Chai

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp tea leaves
  • 1-2 tsp sugar
  • Ginger, cardamom, and clove

Steps

  1. Boil water with ginger, cardamom, and clove.
  2. Add tea leaves and let it simmer.
  3. Pour in milk and sugar.
  4. Boil for a few minutes.
  5. Strain and serve hot.
